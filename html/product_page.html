Behind the Craft

Giving Textiles a Second Life

Every year, millions of tons of fabric scraps and textile leftovers are thrown away. Our community-driven marketplace rescues these discarded materials, routing them directly to skilled local artisans. Through the patient art of hand-embroidery and creative reuse, they transform overlooked wastes into bespoke, narrative-rich home decor and lifestyle items.
